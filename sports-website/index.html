Welcome to your website

You can continue playing the sports you love and developing as an athlete while completing your studies by signing-up for our Sports Academy programme. The Sports Academy is a talent development system designed to help you become the best athlete you can be in your chosen sportThis prime opportunity to remain committed to your sporting life has cemented our reputation as the number one college for sports development locally. Each squad will have the opportunity to train twice-a-week using our extensive facilities, while also playing in county, regional and national competitions throughout the season. Importantly, Sports Academy training schedules and fixtures will be timetabled to fit around your lessons.

Taster Sessions Each year we hold trials where you can discover more about our academies and how you can take part in sport alongside your studies. The Sports Academy system is open to all Brock students. We expect entrants to have a solid background of playing experience and show full commitment to the team, as well as determination to improve.
